## 3. Deliverables (All Tiers)

| # | Deliverable | Owner | Format | Timing |
|---|-------------|-------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Diagnostic intake completed (problem framing, stakeholders, constraints) | Consultant led, with client input | Live session + written summary | Week 1 |
| 2 | AI-assisted diagnostic draft (problem decomposition, hypotheses, root-cause map) | System-generated draft, Consultant validated | Editable document + rationale list | Week 2 |
| 3 | Consultant-signed diagnostic report (final version with amendments, risk flags, decision matrix) | Consultant authored / signed | PDF report + readout deck | Week 3–4 |
| 4 | Action plan & next-step recommendations (roadmap, owner candidates, metrics) | Consultant led | PDF / spreadsheet | Week 3–4 |
| 5 | Knowledge asset record (anonymized) back-written into Phoenix asset system | Phoenix operations | Internal record | Within 1 week of delivery |

**Client receives:** All deliverables in #1–4.  
**Phoenix retains rights to:** Anonymized problem structures, frameworks used, and resulting knowledge assets (#5).

---

## 4. Process & Responsibilities

| Phase | Phoenix Activities | Client Activities | Duration |
|-------|--------------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Kick-off** | Confirm problem statement, success metrics, access to needed contacts | Provide problem owner, internal data where applicable | 30–60 min |
| **Intake** | Conduct structured diagnostic intake interview | 1–2 stakeholders available; provide factual context | 3–5 business days |
| **Drafting** | Run diagnostic system, review output, amend reasoning | Receive draft for comment (5 business days) | 7–10 business days |
| **Validation** | Incorporate client feedback, sign output | Review and confirm factual accuracy | 3–5 business days |
| **Delivery** | Present final report + readout | Executive readout attendance | 1–2 business days |
| **Knowledge Write-back** | Structure anonymized case into asset library | None | Internal only |

Success criteria: Client representative can walk into an internal meeting using the diagnostic output without Phoenix staff present.

## 5. Change Orders

Any change to problem scope, stakeholder set, or report depth after Week 1 kick-off shall be quoted separately and requires mutual written agreement.  
Estimated out-of-scope rates:  
- Additional diagnostic dimension: NT$60,000–100,000  
- Extra facilitated workshop: NT$80,000–120,000  
- Custom follow-on consulting weeks: by separate SOW

---

## 6. Confidentiality

- All client data and stakeholder names are excluded from Phoenix’s knowledge asset records.  
- Only de-identified problem structures, industry category, framework usage, and anonymized outcomes are retained.  
- Client may request deletion of assets post-engagement unless client has provided written consent to retain for model improvement.

---

## 7. Acceptance & Sign-off

**Client accepts deliverables upon:**  
1. Receipt of signed diagnostic report from Phoenix consultant.  
2. Acceptance that drafts are intermediate artifacts; final versions are consultant-signed.  
3. Acknowledgment that anonymized knowledge assets may be used internally.
