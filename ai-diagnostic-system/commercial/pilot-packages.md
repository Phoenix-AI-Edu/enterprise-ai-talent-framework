# Diagnostic Pilot — Client Packages (Sales-Ready)

**Brand Name (internal working title):** HyperSolve  
**Public Positioning:** Enterprise problem-decision diagnostic AI, with consultant-signed accountability  
**Audience:** COO, VP Operations, Chief of Staff, BU Heads (companies NT$500M–$5B revenue, or multinational subsidiaries)

---

## Package Structure

### Lite Diagnostic
**Target:** Single, focused problem; fast close cycle  
**Typical Use Case:** One operational or AI-adoption problem to diagnose and decide whether to proceed  
**Deliverables:**  
- Intake summary and problem statement  
- Diagnostic report (problem decomposition, hypotheses, root-cause map, risks)  
- Consultant readout (written summary, 30-min debrief call)  
**Estimated Effort:** 2 weeks  
**Indicative Price:**  
- Taiwan: NT$150,000–250,000  
- International: US$20,000–35,000  
**When to Use:** First engagement with a client; validate problem seriousness before recommending a larger diagnostic.

---

### Standard Diagnostic
**Target:** Problem + actionable next steps  
**Typical Use Case:** Operational issue requiring both diagnosis and a decision-ready execution roadmap  
**Deliverables:**  
- Intake summary and problem statement  
- Full diagnostic report  
- Execution roadmap (actions, owners, metrics, sequencing)  
- Executive readout deck  
- Consultant readout (60-min executive readout call)  
**Estimated Effort:** 3–4 weeks  
**Indicative Price:**  
- Taiwan: NT$300,000–500,000  
- International: US$40,000–70,000  
**When to Use:** Client has budget and mandate to act; needs a defensible basis before committing resources.

---

### Strategic Diagnostic
**Target:** High-stakes, multi-dimensional problem requiring facilitated alignment  
**Typical Use Case:** Cross-functional or board-level issue where diagnosis alone is insufficient and facilitation is needed to align stakeholders  
**Deliverables:**  
- Standard Diagnostic deliverables  
- Half-day or full-day facilitated workshop (alignment, decision, prioritization)  
- Follow-on consulting days (scope: 4 consultant days for implementation support, governance setup, or tracker build)  
- Final presentation-ready board brief  
**Estimated Effort:** 4–6 weeks  
**Indicative Price:**  
- Taiwan: NT$500,000–800,000  
- International: US$70,000–110,000  
**When to Use:** Board approval or multi-stakeholder commitment required; execution support needed.

---

## Pricing Principles

1. **Pricing by scope, not seats or users.**  
2. **Do not publish standard prices publicly in early stage.** Use “indicative range”; quote per engagement after intake.  
3. **Prefer US$ for multinational clients and subsidiaries of global groups.**  
4. **Bundle strategy workshop + follow-on days into upper tier** to preserve trust and upsell naturally into ongoing advisory.

## Objection Handlers (First Draft)

| Objection | Response Angle |
|-----------|----------------|
| “Why not just use ChatGPT to do this?” | The system applies our proprietary diagnostic frameworks and case-validated patterns, plus every output is consultant-signed for accountability. |
| “How is this different from a traditional management consultant?” | You get framework-rigorous structure at ~40–60% of typical consulting fees, delivered in weeks not months. |
| “What if the diagnosis is wrong?” | Outputs are hypothesis-driven and explicitly risk-flagged; consultant signs final version. You receive a decision-ready document, not a black-box answer. |
| “Our problem is unique.” | Uniqueness is rarely total. We systematically compare your problem structure against prior cases and frameworks; customization is built-in. |
| “Do I need to provide internal data?” | No mandatory data upload. You provide expert inputs and constraints; we work with what exists. |
